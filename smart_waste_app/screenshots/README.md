## How to Add Screenshots

1. Take screenshots from your iPhone/Android device
2. Transfer them to your Mac
3. Rename them according to the structure above
4. Place them in the correct folder
5. Commit and push to GitHub

## Recommended Screenshot Settings

- **Dimensions**: iPhone screenshots work best
- **Format**: PNG for best quality
- **Hide Status Bar**: Optional, cleaner look
- **Dark Mode**: Take separate screenshots for dark mode showcase
